Automated Pre-flight Check

Our processing system automatically flags pixelated files or missing transparency layers before your sheets enter production.

Heat-Press Calibration Support

Every delivery pack includes precise temperature, execution time, and peeling method configuration metrics tailored to your fabric selection.

We accept PNG, TIFF, PSD, AI, and PDF vector formats. For raster graphics (like PNG), ensure your file resolution is explicitly set to a minimum of 300 DPI with a completely transparent background to prevent clipping defects or blurred edges.

For standard cotton and poly-blend apparel, press at 150°C to 160°C (302°F to 320°F) under medium-to-firm pressure for 12 to 15 seconds. Allow the transfer to cool down completely before initiating our clean cold peel extraction process.
